Getting Started

Born To Blog is a digital media platform designed specifically for bloggers, journalists, writers, and content creators. We provide tools for publishing articles, building an audience, and connecting with a community of storytellers. Our platform is built with a focus on African voices and perspectives, though writers from anywhere are welcome.

Creating an account is simple and free. Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage, enter your email address, choose a username, and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration. Once registered, you can start publishing immediately.

Yes! Born To Blog is free for all writers. You can create an account, publish unlimited articles, and build your audience without paying anything. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core publishing and community features will always be free.

Writing & Publishing

After logging in, click the "Write" button in your dashboard. This opens our editor where you can write your article, add images, format text, and more. When you're ready, click "Publish" to make your article live. You can also save drafts to continue working later.

You can publish a wide variety of content including blog posts, news articles, opinion pieces, tutorials, creative writing, and more. We support text, images, embedded videos, and other media. Yes, you have full control over your content. You can edit your articles at any time to fix errors, update information, or improve your writing. You can also unpublish articles to remove them from public view, or delete them permanently if needed.

You retain full ownership of all content you create and publish on Born To Blog. By publishing on our platform, you grant us a license to display and distribute your content, but you can remove it at any time. You're also free to republish your content elsewhere.

Account & Profile

Go to your Settings page to customize your profile. You can add a profile photo, write a bio, include your location, and link to your social media accounts. A complete profile helps readers connect with you and builds trust in your writing.

If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Yes, you can delete your account at any time from your Settings page. When you delete your account, your profile and all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Please note that this action cannot be undone, so make sure to export any content you want to keep.

Mobile App

Yes! The Born To Blog app is available for both Android and iOS devices. You can download it from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. The app includes all the features of the web platform plus offline writing capability and push notifications.

Yes, our mobile app supports offline writing. You can compose and edit articles without an internet connection. Your work is saved locally and will automatically sync when you're back online. This is perfect for writing on the go or in areas with unreliable internet.

Community & Support

Born To Blog has several community features. You can follow other writers, comment on articles, and participate in discussions. We also host occasional community events, writing challenges, and workshops. Follow us on social media to stay updated on community activities.

If you encounter content that violates our Community Guidelines, you can report it by clicking the three-dot menu on any article and selecting "Report." Provide a brief explanation of the issue, and our team will review it within 24-48 hours. We take reports seriously and act on violations promptly.

You can reach our support team by emailing support@borntoblog.com or by using the contact form on our Contact page. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, include "URGENT" in your subject line.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
