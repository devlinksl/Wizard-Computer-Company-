About Us

Your Complete Technology Destination

Wizard's Computer Company SL Ltd is a full-service technology business based in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to making technology accessible, practical, and affordable for everyone.

Our Story

Sierra Leone's #1 Computer Shop

Wizard's Computer Company SL Ltd is proud to be recognized as the #1 computer shop in Sierra Leone, offering top-quality computers, laptops, gaming PCs, office systems, and accessories. Since our founding, we’ve been committed to bringing the best technology solutions to individuals, students, and businesses across Freetown and beyond.

From fully equipped retail outlets to hands-on learning programs, we combine expert advice, unbeatable products, and reliable support. Our dedicated team ensures that every customer finds efficient, budget-friendly technology tailored to their needs, making us the leading destination for computers and accessories in Sierra Leone.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be Sierra Leone's #1 computer shop by providing high-quality computers, laptops, gaming PCs, office systems, and accessories. We aim to empower individuals, students, and businesses with reliable technology solutions and expert guidance for success in the digital world.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted technology partner in Sierra Leone, where everyone can access premium computers, accessories, and professional IT support. We strive to lead the market in quality, service, and innovation, setting the standard for computer shops nationwide.
