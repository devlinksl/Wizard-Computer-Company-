About Us

Your Complete Technology Destination

Wizard's Computer Company SL Ltd is a full-service technology business based in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to making technology accessible, practical, and affordable for everyone.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Wizard's Computer Company SL Ltd was founded with a simple but powerful idea: that everyone in Sierra Leone deserves access to quality technology, training, and support. What started as a small computer cafe has grown into a comprehensive technology hub serving hundreds of individuals and businesses across Freetown.

Today, we operate a full-service computer cafe, a hands-on learning center offering structured training programs, and a retail outlet stocked with quality computers and accessories. Our team of experienced IT professionals is committed to empowering our community with the digital skills and tools they need to succeed.

Our Location

174 Bai Bureh Rd, Portee, Wellington Freetown, Sierra Leone. Open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Walk-ins welcome for cafe services, product purchases, and training enrollment.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses in Sierra Leone with the digital skills, tools, and support they need to thrive in the modern economy. We bridge the digital skills gap by providing accessible and practical technology solutions.

Our Vision

To become Sierra Leone's most trusted technology partner, where every individual and business has access to quality computer training, reliable equipment, and expert IT support to compete in the digital economy.

What We Do

Three Pillars of Service

Computer Cafe

Access high-speed internet, print documents, and use modern workstations in a comfortable, professional environment. Open daily for walk-in customers, students, and professionals who need reliable computing access.

Learning Center

Join structured training programs in computer basics, Microsoft Office, programming fundamentals, and more. Our courses are designed for beginners and working professionals who want to build practical digital skills.

Device Retail & Support

From laptops and desktops to monitors and accessories, we sell, set up, and support a wide range of computer equipment for personal and business use. Every product is quality-tested and backed by our warranty.
